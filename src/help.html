How to use it:

This is simple app to sum points in Carcassone boardgame.

How it works: -Click on square colored (almost) with Your meep (thats how pawn is called in this game) color. -Then u make it active wich is indicated with letter A. -Now when You click on blue buttons from 1 to 10 You add to your score that amount of points. -If you make mistake or find easier to remove some points from one of player click button "Subtract" that make sum buttons to remove score. -If You want to clear table double click (for safe) Clear button. -You can change multiplication of buttons by clicking on multiplication buttons.
